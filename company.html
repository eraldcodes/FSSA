Company

FINANCIAL SECURITY SOFTWEAR ALBANIAN develops software solutions that provide immediate benefits to financial institutions. we are able to offer fast and effective global support to all of our customers.

In addition to software development, we also provide:

  • Consultancy services that supply clients with the knowledge to improve their business processes
  • Project management expertise
  • Responsive and friendly software support

Stability and Experience

We have been working on our software products since 2007. We offer the reliability and experience of a company with a proven track record. Clients in sixteen countries on four continents use our software to meet their business needs, including some of the world's largest financial institutions.

Long Term Vision

Our aim at FSSA is to create software products that are superior in every way and sell these into every sector of the financial markets. The FSSA brand is a reflection of the quality, honesty and innovation we deliver.
