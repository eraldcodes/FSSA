4sight Repo System

Repo Software for the Full Trade Lifecycle

The 4sight Repo system provides front to back office Repo software for faster processing of repo transactions. It supports trade entry through to collateral management and settlement.

The 4sight Repo solution also helps to reduce the strain on operations with the ability to process many repo trade lifecycle events in bulk. Straight through processing is enhanced through interfaces with electronic trading platforms and 4sight Repo includes a built in SWIFT processor with full settlement tracking.

4sight's Repo module also offers collateral management and collateral optimisation modules that guide the user through the margining process and enable real time views of exposures and collateral inventory across business lines.

4sight Repo System Features

  • Repo and multi collateral repo, buy sell backs
  • Tri-Party repo
  • ECN connectivity
  • Intelligent views of availability and short positions
  • Realtime position management
  • Configurable MIS and reporting toolkit
  • Reinvestment trade support
  • Real-time repo collateral and margin management
  • Easy integration with third party systems

Repo System Benefits

  • Lower Costs
  • Quicker trade and lifecycle execution
  • Reduced risk
  • Greater STP
  • Higher trading volumes
  • Improved decision making
