The Complete Web Reporting Solution

In today's demanding global trading environment, organisations rely on accurate, relevant and timely information from Securities Finance and Collateral Management systems to make critical business decisions.

4sight meets these demands through its Web Reporting module, which allows you to extract the data you need from the 4sight Securities Finance and Collateral Management solutions, manipulate and present it in the format you require. If necessary, you can also drill down to view the underlying data.

Improve transparency

4sight Web Reporting provides a suite of standard reports that you can use as is, customise yourself, or add to with your own reports. You can personalise reports with your organisation's corporate colours and logo, allowing them to be used for client reporting or even white labelling.

You can also give your clients access to run and schedule their own queries and reports, improving service levels, transparency and client satisfaction.

Stock Lending Open Trades Report in the 4sight Web Reporting module

Create any kind of report

The module utilises Crystal Reports, enabling you to create virtually any kind of report required, including charts, cross tabs and grid reports through an intuitive interface. Reports can display summary calculations at appropriate break points and calculations can be added to extend the data retrieved.

4sight Web Reporting is based on a 'Windows' compliant user interface and you can access it from any PC using a standard internet browser. This ensures users feel familiar with the system from the outset. You can also save reports in a wide variety of formats including PDF and Excel.

Automate your reporting

You can print reports according to a predefined timetable that you can configure, removing the need to request the same set of reports each morning. Scheduling can take account of daily, weekly and monthly reporting requirements and is easy to set up, with the option of automated email reports sent out to key staff.

Cross system reporting

4sight Web Reporting can incorporate data from other systems into its reports, including third party securities lending solutions.

Because the module is independent of the main 4sight Securities Finance solution, departments that are not core users but require data from the system, for instance; Management, Accounting, Risk, and Audit Compliance can also receive the latest reports. This ensures they obtain the information they need without installation of the core securities finance application on their desktop.
