Collateral Management in Asia Webinar: The Transition Towards a Holistic Model

Please enter your details to the right to receive the webinar recording.

Collateral Management for firms trading in Asia is rapidly changing today. Regulatory requirements originating from BCBS-IOSCO are driving regional market participants to consider changing their business models and methodologies along with moving towards the application of more sophisticated collateral techniques.

This webinar discusses some of the key market and regulatory trends globally and how they impact Asian firms on both the buyside and sellside.

It also covers some of the technical changes required in order to support a more challenging regulatory environment with new holistic models designed to manage and optimise collateral.


Title: Collateral Management in Asia: The Transition Towards a Holistic Model
Date: Thursday, 18th February 2016
Time: 4pm Hong Kong/Singapore Time/ 8am GMT

Agenda:

1. Factors driving change in Asian collateral management.
2. Key challenges to market participants today.
3. Healthcheck: Where are you in meeting the challenges?
4 Future Trends: Holistic financing models and integrated cross product solutions
5. Optimisation: How far should you go?


Our Presenters:


Sam Ahmed is Managing Director of DerivAsia. His career in banking spans 18 years across top tier banks in the AsiaPac region. Prior to launching DerivAsia, Sam was heading Citi's Collateral Services Sales for the Asia Pac Rim region where he was both instrumental in developing innovative solutions as well as originating sales strategy in the region.

Sinead O'Sullivan is Director of Business Solutions at 4sight. She has 10 years' Securities Finance and collateral management experience, having previously worked at UBS and Barclays. Sinead joined 4sight in 2012, and has recently been working with clients and prospects to define solutions across the collateral management and synthetic finance markets.

Martin Seagroatt is 4sight's Director of Marketing and Product Innovation. Prior to working at 4sight he worked in energy risk management. In his ten years at 4sight, he has specialised in securities lending, repo and OTC/listed derivatives collateral management.

Paul Wilson is Product Director for 4sight and has 10 years' securities finance and collateral management experience. Paul has worked closely with many 4sight clients and prospects in a pre-sales and business analysis role and was instrumental in the design of 4sight's collateral management and collateral optimisation solutions.
