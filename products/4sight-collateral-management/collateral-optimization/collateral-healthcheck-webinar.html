Webinar: How to Benchmark and Improve Your Collateral Management

Please enter your details to the right to receive the webinar recording.

Collateral management is changing rapidly; working out how to respond can be challenging. The webinar, held on Oct 7th 2015 discusses how to benchmark your current collateral systems and processes against best practice.

Title: How to Benchmark and Improve Your Collateral Management

Date: Wednesday, 7th October 2015

Agenda:

1. Factors driving change in collateral systems and processes

2. What is the optimal collateral operating model?

3. Costs, risks and flexibility

4. Benchmarking your current systems and processes

5. Identifying realistic goals

Presenters:

Martin Seagroatt is 4sight's Director of Marketing and Product Innovation. In his ten years at 4sight, he has specialised in securities lending, repo and OTC/listed derivatives collateral management.

Ed Cockram is 4sight's North American Product Consulting Director. Ed has extensive collateral management experience, gained from roles at ABN Amro and Fortis Bank.

Sinead O'Sullivan is Director of Business Solutions at 4sight. She has 10 years' Securities Finance experience, having previously worked at UBS and Barclays. Sinead joined 4sight in 2012, and has recently been working with clients and prospects to define solutions across the collateral management and synthetic finance markets.

About 4sight

4sight Financial Software is a leading supplier of innovative software solutions for Collateral Management, Securities Finance, Swaps, Settlement and Market Connectivity.

Paul Wilson is Product Director for 4sight and has 10 years' securities finance and collateral management experience. Paul has worked closely with many 4sight clients and prospects in a pre-sales and business analysis role and was instrumental in the design of 4sight's collateral management and optimization solutions.
