SEB Equities Goes Live with 4sight’s Settlement Software

SEB Equities has successfully expanded its use of 4sight's Oneworld Settlement system for its Derivatives business.

The settlement system will enable SEB to process domestic and non-domestic equity and derivative trades on Oslo, Eurex and Nasdaq OMX exchanges.

SEB had used 4sight's settlement solution for a number of years for equities trading but wanted to replace multiple legacy settlement systems across different business lines with a single system.

SEB evaluated 4sight against a number of vendor solutions available in the market. SEB selected 4sight because of the strength of 4sight's customer support, extensive project management experience and ability to quickly develop support for new product types and market connections.

The project involved adding the settlement of new product types to the 4sight OneWorld Settlement system, establishing connectivity with a number of CCPs and data migration from SEB's legacy systems.

Alastair Chisholm, 4sight's Managing Director comments; "We are delighted with the success of the implementation and the close working relationship we have developed with SEB. This project adds to the growing use of 4sight's software products in Nordic region, with a number of Swedish banks now using 4sight's settlement, collateral management and securities finance solutions."

"We were very happy with the project", comments Sven Andersson, Head of Electronic Execution Equities SEB. "The 4sight system will enable us to increase trading volumes as our business expands and achieve significant operational efficiencies from using a single system solution to manage our clearing and settlement process across multiple business lines."
